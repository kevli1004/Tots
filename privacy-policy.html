Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025
Your Privacy Matters: Grow with Tots is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your family's data should remain private and secure.

1. Information We Collect

Grow with Tots collects and processes the following information to provide our baby tracking services:

  • Baby Information: Name, birth date, and growth measurements
  • Activity Data: Feeding times, diaper changes, sleep patterns, pumping sessions, milestones, and learned words
  • Health Data: Weight, height, head circumference for growth tracking
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns to improve user experience
  • Device Information: Device type and iOS version for compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Track your baby's daily activities and development
  • Generate growth charts and milestone tracking
  • Provide personalized insights and recommendations
  • Send activity reminders and notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Local-First Approach: All your data is primarily stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account.
  • Local Storage: Data is stored securely on your device using iOS security features
  • iCloud Integration: When enabled, data syncs to your personal iCloud account using Apple's secure infrastructure
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption
  • No Third-Party Servers: We do not store your personal data on third-party servers

4. Third-Party Services

Grow with Tots integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device and usage information for ad personalization. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Services: iCloud for data sync, App Store for app distribution, and iOS system services

5. Data Retention

  • Data is retained as long as you use the app
  • You can delete individual records or all data at any time through the app
  • When you delete the app, all local data is removed
  • iCloud data follows Apple's iCloud retention policies

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Export your data in CSV format
  • Delete individual records or all data
  • Disable iCloud sync
  • Control notification and tracking preferences

7. Age Requirements

16+ Only: Grow with Tots is intended for use by individuals who are at least 16 years of age. The app is designed for parents, guardians, and caregivers to track their babies and children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from someone under 16, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

If you are under 16 years of age, please do not use this app or provide any personal information. Parents and guardians are responsible for monitoring their children's use of mobile devices and ensuring compliance with this age requirement.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and through the app when appropriate.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: support@growwithtots.com

Website: growwithtots.com

Commitment to Privacy: We are committed to protecting your family's privacy and will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
